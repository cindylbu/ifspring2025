Recipe Page

This website showcases the recipe for Gyūdon ("beef bowl"), a traditional Japanese comfort dish. The design emphasizes intuitive, step-by-step instructions and includes features that help users easily identify dietary restrictions, ensuring a smooth and inclusive cooking experience.

Microsite - Creative Director

Serving the role of a Creative Director, this website worked with Designer Alexandra Savin to achieve the goal of informing users about ramen as a cultural symbol of Japan. The site highlights its regional evolution and modern relevance through clean, minimal aesthetics inspired by traditional Japanese design.

Microsite - Designer/Developer

Serving the role of a Designer, this site was designed under the creative direction of Victoria Prisco. It presents the history, core branches, and methods of philosophy in a clean, neutral, and accessible format for anyone interested in the field.
